About Vihltara

Vihltara is an upcoming role-playing game that is being made in Godot.
The game will take place in the kingdom of Rusana, where you'll have the freedom to explore however you want.

The development history of Vihltara (coming soon)

Multiple events, all happening simultaneously

I - World of chaos

Rusana is filled with regions and inhabitants that have appeared from unknown realms.
As the rifts from these realms form less and less frequently, dangerous beings begin to appear. Possessing powers that could lead to the complete destruction of Rusana.

II - An enemy hidden in plain sight

Queen Reichi Sana is known to have an extremely dangerous amount of magical energy and abilities that make her almost godlike.
Her appearance, despite being a Krovihl, has raised concern as to who she really is.
Even though her actions over the years have been heroic and helped Rusana prosper, many have begun to question the young Queen.

III - Rachel

Several explorers from Senteira have been entering newly discovered rifts and found artifacts that mention someone from an unknown timeline who bears resemblence to Reichi and Elka.
Although they do not mention any of the sisters, the explorers suspect that one of them may have been the reason for many of the recent events.

IV - The cursed elves

Krovihls have abilities similar to that of the Ancients. However, they are unable to control their powers and have been causing unintended destruction in regions outside of Krobuta.
Upon successfully entering a secret area in Alfira, an adventurer from Sal Vana learns that the elves have been capturing many Krovihl and draining their magical energy to power weapons that could be used to rebel against the other regions of Rusana.

V - Realm of the Ancients

The long forgotten guardians of Rusana have become active once again. Originally created to protect the nation from the Ancients, many fear that the long extinct race could be returning to Rusana.
